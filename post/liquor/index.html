Liquor Cabinet

The Liquor Cabinet was founded in 2015 by brothers Patrick, Peter, and Sean Janelle to be the authoritative source for everything related to spirits and the art of cocktails.

The mobile app is an on-the-go guide and spirit shop for the modern drinker, including an exclusive collection of recipes curated and tested by TLC in-house expert, Maxwell Britten, and a detailed bottle database filled with helpful insights about individual bottles of liquor.
